CauliflowerPies

Description

CauliflowerPies recipe for ketomaniacs

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Cauliflower 400g
  • Salt and pepper
  • Baking soda 5g
  • Onion 50g
  • Mk Gold 50g

Steps

  1. Raw cauliflower and onion grate, add eggs and add MK Gold Bread Mix and baking powder, season with salt and pepper as desired
  2. Mix everything thoroughly.
  3. Fry in any fat
