Ketocookies

Description

Ketocookies recipe for ketomaniacs

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Butter 30g
  • Erytrytol 50g
  • Baking soda 5g
  • Almond flour 50g
  • Dark chocolate 50g
  • Peanut butter 50g

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, add peanut butter to it, thoroughly.
  2. Add erythritol and egg, add dry ingredients to the mass prepared in this way, mix all ingredients thoroughly with a mixer.
  3. Form balls, then flatten into cookie shapes
  4. Bake at 180'C on a grid and baking paper, baking time depends on the size of the cookies
