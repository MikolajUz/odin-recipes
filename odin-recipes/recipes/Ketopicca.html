Picca

Description

MK-gold pizza crust recipe for ketomaniacs

Ingredients

  • Olives oil 40g
  • Honey 5g
  • MK Gold 200g
  • Dry yeast 5g
  • Water 220g

Steps

  1. Water up yeast until there'll be seen bubbles
  2. Add rest of ingredients
  3. Mis it up with blender
  4. Leave to grow for 5 minutes
